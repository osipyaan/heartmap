Smart 3D-Engineered Cardiac Patch

An AI-guided solution to support heart regeneration

Why This Matters

Cardiovascular diseases, particularly heart attacks and arrhythmias, affect millions. Our bioengineered patch, enhanced with AI-guided electrical mapping, aims to support heart tissue regeneration and restore proper function.

The Innovation

  • 3D-printed bio-based polymer scaffold designed to match the heart’s geometry
  • Integrated AI models that predict conductivity pathways and optimize patch placement
  • Supports synchronized electrical signaling to reduce risk of arrhythmias
