Hybrid Engineered Arrhythmia-Resistant Regenerative Tissue with Machine learning-Augmented Placement

An AI-guided solution to support heart regeneration

you are at risk of cardiovascular disease, particularly heart attacks and arrhythmias. this bioengineered patch, enhanced with AI-guided electrical mapping, aims to support heart tissue regeneration and restore proper function.

  • Integrated AI models that predict conductivity pathways and optimize patch placement
